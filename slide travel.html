kerala

ts lush unspoiled tropical beauty, exotic locales and tranquil backwaters attract travelers from across the world who seeks leisurely vacation. Apart from its picturesque scenery, Kerala is also known for its rich wildlife and elaborate temples.

Tajmahal

The Taj Mahal is a perfect symmetrical planned building, with an emphasis of bilateral symmetry along a central axis on which the main features are placed. The building material used is brick-in-lime mortar veneered with red sandstone and marble and inlay work of precious/semi precious stones.

JAMMU&KASHMIR

Home to some of the country's most picturesque attractions, Jammu and Kashmir is a must-see for every kind of traveller. It's a place where nature's beauty shines brightest, and is surrounded by snow-capped mountains and gleaming lakes, making it a haven of peace.

lotustemple

The Lotus Temple in New Delhi is one of 7 temples of the Baha'i faith in the world. Like all Baha'i temples its structure is a circular 9-sided shape. The building is made up of 27 free-standing marble petals arranged to form the 9 sides of the temple..
